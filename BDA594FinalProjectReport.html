Problem Statement:

Healthcare Accessibility in San Diego County: A Geospatial and Socioeconomic Analysis.

Our project aims to evaluate the accessibility of various tiers of healthcare services in San Diego County. We will examine the density and accessibility using various factors within available geospatial and socioeconomic datasets. Our examination looks at various geographical aspects including location, distances to facilities, regional geography, and transportation methods. This will be compared to research the potential disparities in healthcare access across various socioeconomic variables. The purpose of this study is to create data-driven insights that can influence policy and decisions to improve equitable healthcare access within the county, and hopefully with further analysis and refined data methodology, beyond that. To execute this goal we will be utilizing both data gathering and geospatial mapping methods to determine correlations and patterns that can provide insight into these inequities and their impacts on various population groups.
